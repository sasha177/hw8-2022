Birth

Born on February 16, 1990

First Upload

Uploaded "Loft Music", "What You Need", and "The Morning" on Youtube in December 2010 with the username "xoxxxoooxo"

First Album

Released "Trilogy" which became #4 on the Billboards in 2012

Kiss Land

In 2013, released his album "Kiss Land" which reached #2 on the U.S. Billboard 200, and he sold 273,000 copies of the album

Collabs

Remixed "Drunk in Love" with Rihanna in 2014, duetted Arianna Grande for "Love Me Harder", realeased a song for Fifty Shades of Grey titled "Earned It"

Centric Award

Won a Centric Award in 2015 for his song "Earned It", which he performed with Alicia Keys

Starboy

In 2016, released "Starboy", an album that remained at #1 on U.S. Billoards 200 for quite a while.

Grammy Award

Received a Grammy nomiation for the 60th Annual Grammy for Best Urban Contemporary Album for his album "Starboy" in 2017 (and won!).

After Hours Til Dawn

In 2022, went on tour for two combined albums "After Hours" and "Dawn FM" after pushing back his tour earlier in the year
